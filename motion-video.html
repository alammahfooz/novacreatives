Motion Graphics & Video Editing

This list covers a range of services related to animated visuals and video production, crucial
for a modern business's marketing and communication efforts.

1. Foundational & Branding

  • Logo Animation: Creating dynamic, short animations of a brand’s logo for video intros and outros.
  • Animated Titles & Lower Thirds: Designing on-brand animated text overlays for context and speaker identification.
  • Brand Style Guide for Motion: Documenting animation speed, transitions, and sound cues to maintain consistency.

2. Marketing & Social Media Video

  • Explainer Videos: Animated videos that simplify complex ideas, products, or services.
  • Short-Form Video Production: High-engagement vertical videos for Reels, TikTok, and Shorts.
  • Social Media Ad Creative: Designing and animating compelling video advertisements.
  • Video Testimonials: Editing branded customer testimonial videos to build trust.
  • Animated Infographics: Turning data and statistics into engaging animated visuals.

3. Corporate & Internal Video

  • Corporate Videos: Professional videos for internal communication and company culture.
  • Presentation & Event Videos: Animated slides, intro videos, and event highlight reels.
  • Training & E-learning Modules: Visually engaging videos for employee training.

4. Advanced & Specialty

  • 2D and 3D Animation: Custom animations including characters and product visualization.
  • Compositing & Visual Effects (VFX): Seamlessly combining multiple visual elements.
  • Motion Tracking: Graphics and text that follow object movement in video.
  • Sound Design & Scoring: Custom sound effects and music to enhance emotional impact.
