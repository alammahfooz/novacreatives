Social Media Design Services

This list covers all the essential design services for social media, from a basic brand
presence to advanced content and advertising.

1. Foundational & Profile Design

  • Profile Picture & Avatar Design: Creating branded images optimized for each platform.
  • Cover & Banner Art: Designing headers for Facebook, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, and YouTube.
  • Highlight Covers (Instagram): Creating cohesive Story Highlight icons.
  • Channel Branding (YouTube): Banner, thumbnails, and end-screen designs.

2. Organic Content Design

  • Static Post Templates: Reusable, on-brand templates for daily posting.
  • Infographics & Data Visuals: Visually presenting complex information.
  • Quote Graphics: Custom visuals for quotes, testimonials, and brand messages.
  • Story & Reel Templates: Vertical content templates for Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok.
  • Branded GIFs & Stickers: Custom animated assets for platforms like Giphy.

3. Paid Advertising Design

  • Social Media Ad Creative: High-conversion visuals for paid campaigns.
  • Carousel Ad Design: Multi-image ads showcasing products or storytelling.
  • Retargeting Ad Graphics: Ads for audiences who already engaged.
  • Video Ad Production: Short-form videos and motion graphics for ads.

4. Custom & Special Projects

  • Campaign-Specific Content: Full visual packages for launches or contests.
  • Event & Live Stream Graphics: Overlays, banners, and countdown screens.
  • Influencer Collaboration Assets: Custom templates for influencer promotions.
  • Audit & Strategy: Reviewing existing visuals and defining improvement strategies.
