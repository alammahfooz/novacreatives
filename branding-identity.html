Branding & Identity Design Services

This list details the foundational and ongoing branding services a design firm can offer to help
businesses establish a unique and memorable identity.

1. Core Identity Design

  • Logo Design: Creating unique, scalable, and memorable logos that serve as the primary visual mark of the brand.
  • Brand Style Guide: A comprehensive document outlining rules for logo usage, color palettes, typography, and imagery.
  • Color Palette Development: Selecting primary, secondary, and accent colors that evoke desired brand emotions.
  • Typography Selection: Choosing font families optimized for both digital and print use.

2. Visual System & Asset Creation

  • Iconography: Designing a custom icon set for websites, apps, and marketing materials.
  • Illustrative Style: Developing a unique illustration style for storytelling.
  • Photography & Imagery Guidelines: Defining lighting, composition, and subject matter.
  • Visual Language: Establishing patterns, textures, and graphic elements.

3. Brand Application & Collateral

  • Stationery Design: Business cards, letterheads, and envelopes.
  • Packaging Design: Product boxes, labels, and bags aligned with brand identity.
  • Merchandise Design: Branded t-shirts, mugs, and notebooks.
  • Social Media Branding: Profile visuals, covers, and post templates.
  • Email Signature Design: Professional signatures for all team members.

4. Brand Strategy & Audits

  • Brand Audit: Identifying strengths, weaknesses, and improvement areas.
  • Naming & Tagline Development: Brainstorming memorable brand names and taglines.
  • Mission & Vision Statement: Crafting statements that guide brand direction.
